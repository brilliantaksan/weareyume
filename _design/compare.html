Hero A — Logo Card
Centered · Symmetric
Closest to the brand mark. Big centered "Yu+Me" floating on a cloud of gradient blobs, with white crayon scribbles scattered around — essentially the profile picture extended into a full hero. Reads as: identity-first, gentle, welcoming.
  • Best for: first-time visitors who need to grok the brand
  • Tradeoff: less information density above the fold
Hero B — Editorial Spread
Asymmetric · Magazine
Zine / magazine feel. Yu+Me wordmark on the left, photo collage with pink crayon date stamps on the right, key info (where/when/cost) below the fold, and a marquee of past session dates at the bottom. Reads as: active community, lots of receipts, drop in this Saturday.
  • Best for: repeat visitors checking the next date
  • Tradeoff: heavier; more attention required
